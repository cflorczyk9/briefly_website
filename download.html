Add Briefly to Claude.

Briefly runs inside the Claude desktop app, on your firm's own files. Download the connector, double-click it, and you are set up in about five minutes.

Download Briefly ↓ For Mac and Windows.
Requires the Claude desktop app.
Briefly runs on a firm seat. The connector installs for anyone and briefs build on your own machine. To turn on your firm's shared learning layer, you sign in with a seat we set up for you. Not set up yet? Request access and we will provision your firm.

Five minutes to your first brief.

STEP 01

Install

Double-click briefly-mcp.mcpb. The Claude desktop app opens and asks to install Briefly. Click Install. If it offers to set up Node.js, allow it.

STEP 02

Point Briefly at your folder

Pick your firm's folder, enter your firm's short name, and your own advisor identifier. Leave the folder blank and Briefly makes a starter one for you.

STEP 03

Give Briefly a document

Drop a client file in, or hand it to Briefly in chat: Add this to the Smith family's file. Briefly reads it and builds that client's profile.

STEP 04

Ask for a brief

Ask: Prepare a meeting brief for the Smith family. Briefly builds it from your files, with a source you can click behind every line.

Works in Claude Desktop on macOS and Windows · Your client files stay on your machine

Now switch to Claude

Briefly just downloaded and is opening in the Claude desktop app. Switch to Claude Desktop and click Install to finish.
