Five minutes to your first brief.

STEP 01

Get the free Claude app

Briefly runs inside Claude, Anthropic's free desktop app. Get Claude, then come back.

STEP 02

Install Briefly

Double-click briefly_wealth.mcpb, then click Install in Claude. That's the whole install: nothing to type, no account form. You only do this once.

STEP 03

Sign in with your firm login

The settings screen you'll see is optional: leave it blank and Briefly creates its folder for you. When Claude asks, sign in with the login we set up for your firm. That login is the whole setup, it tells Briefly who you are and which firm you belong to.

STEP 04

Add client documents

Save statements into your Briefly folder's intake and say File everything in my intake folder. Briefly reads each document, asks who it belongs to, and files the original. Attaching a file in chat works too.

STEP 05

Ask for a brief

Ask Prepare a meeting brief for the Johnson family. Every fact gets a clickable source.

Your original files stay on your machine. Briefly saves them in your folder, builds every brief locally, and never uploads your files. The finished brief is written by your own Claude, so its content follows your Claude plan and privacy settings. How we handle data
