Install walkthrough

Four steps to your first brief.

01

Get Claude

Anthropic's free desktop app. Get Claude, then come back.

02

Install Briefly

Download, then double-click briefly_wealth.mcpb and click Install in Claude. Nothing to type, no account form.

The settings screen is optional: leave it blank and Briefly creates its folder. Advanced: point it at your firm's OneDrive or SharePoint folder.

03

Sign in once

The first time you use Briefly, Claude opens a sign-in window. Use the login Briefly set up for your firm.

That login is the whole setup: it tells Briefly who you are and which firm you belong to.

04

File, then brief

Save statements into your Briefly folder's intake and say “File everything in my intake folder.” Briefly reads each document, asks who it belongs to, and files the original where every citation can open it.

Then ask: “Prepare a meeting brief for the Johnson family.”
